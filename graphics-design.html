Website Logo

Gestalt Principle of Figure and Ground is used as the tube of the spectacle may look like the letter "M" which refers to the word "Mobile" which best describes my dream job which is a mobile app developer.

Name Logo

Balance Principle of Design is used with different sizes on both left and right side of the logo to implement asymmetrical balance.

Gestalt Principle of Continuity is used to show the viewer that the logo name should be read from left to right.

Gestalt Principle of Closure is used with curved lines for the edge and rectangle or the letter which represents the logo name to form the pair of spectacles.

Instagram Post

Emphasis Principle of Design is used by having the text "Travelly Now!" being the largest text to play with the company name by telling the viewers to travel now.

Gestalt Principle of Continuity is used to attract the viewers to look at the poster from the top to bottom.

Electronic Direct Mail Poster

Emphasis Principle of Design is used to allow viewers to see the text "EX" at first glance. It makes use of the Gestalt Principles of Closure by having an arrow to show viewers to read the word "Excel".

Gestalt Principle of Figure and Ground is used to give an illusion to viewers since the text "ab" forms the "Responsible consumption and production" logo.

Balance Principle of Design is also used by having a gradient background.

Food and Drink Wireframe

Auto Layout is used in buttons, text fields, bottom navigation bar, rating and food category icons, food name with price as well as profile information with edit Icons for responsiveness.

"On Tap", "Touch Down" and "While Pressing" triggers are used for users to interact with the food and drink application.
