Name Logo

Balance Principle of Design is used with different sizes on both left and right side of the logo to implement asymmetrical balance.

Gestalt Principle of Continuity is used to show the viewer that the logo name should be read from left to right.

Gestalt Principle of Closure is used with curved lines for the edge and rectangle or the letter which represents the logo name to form the pair of spectacles.
